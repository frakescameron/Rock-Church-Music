What To Expect

Your First Visit

We want your first visit to feel simple and welcoming. You do not need to dress a certain way or have everything figured out before you come. We are a church made up of real people walking with Jesus together.

You can expect worship, Bible-based teaching, prayer, and a church family that wants to love people well.

FAQ

Common Questions

What should I wear?

Come as you are. We want people to feel comfortable and welcome.

Do you have something for kids?

Yes. Nursery and children’s church are available on Sunday mornings.

What is a service like?

Our services include worship, prayer, teaching from the Bible, and time together as a church family.

Do I need to know anything before visiting?

No. Just come in, say hello, and let us help you get settled.

How can I talk to someone?

You can contact us by phone or email, or speak with someone after a Sunday service.

How do I stay updated?

Check the calendar page, attend on Sundays, and stay connected with church announcements.
