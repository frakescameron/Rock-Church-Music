Before service, people often gather to talk, catch up, and spend time together. It’s a relaxed space to meet others and feel at home.

We usually have coffee and bagels available—nothing fancy, just a simple way to slow down and connect before the service begins.

FAQ

Common Questions

What should I wear?

Come as you are. We want people to feel comfortable and welcome. Be modest in your attire.

Do you have something for kids?

Yes. Nursery and children’s church are available on Sunday mornings.

What is a service like?

Our services include worship, teaching from the Bible, prayer, and time together as a church family.

Do I need to know anything before visiting?

No. Just come in, say hello, and let us help you get settled.

How can I talk to someone?

You can contact us by phone or email, or speak with someone after a Sunday service.

How do I stay updated?

Check the calendar page, attend on Sundays, and stay connected with church announcements.
